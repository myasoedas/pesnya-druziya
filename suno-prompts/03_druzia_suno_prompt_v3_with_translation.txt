Upbeat and catchy K-POP Dance Pop with Russian Teen Pop influence, designed for kids and teens aged 7–18, optimized for Yappy short videos. Duration about 2 minutes 28 seconds, C Major, BPM 110.

Bright, cheerful, friendly atmosphere with a sense of unity and joy. Vocals: teen female lead with clear pronunciation, smiling tone, and child backing vocals in choruses. Singing in Russian, but provide English translation in prompt for rhythm guidance. Energetic and positive delivery.

Song structure:
Intro — clap + laughter + female teen voice saying "Друзья мы все дружны!" ("Friends, we are all united!"), short pause before beat drops.
Verse 1 — semi-spoken with smile, bright and rhythmic:
"Друг — это ты и я, (Friend — it's you and me,)
Вместе всегда семья. (Together always a family.)
Смеёмся и поём, (We laugh and we sing,)
Всегда вдвоём-втроём!" (Always two or three of us!) 
Chorus 1 — full voice, layered harmonies, repeating hook line three times:
"Друзья мы все дружны, и я, и ты, и ты! (Friends, we are all united, and me, and you, and you!)
Друзья мы все дружны, всегда добры! (Friends, we are all united, always kind!)
Друзья мы все дружны, и я, и ты, и ты! (Friends, we are all united, and me, and you, and you!)
Друзья — это семья, и мы одни!" (Friends are family, and we are one!) 
Verse 2 — playful and friendly:
"Друг может пошутить, (A friend can make a joke,)
И горой за тебя стоять. (And stand like a mountain for you.)
Я знаю, если грустно — (I know, if you're sad —)
Ты рядом, чтоб меня обнять!" (You're here to give me a hug!) 
Chorus 2 — same as Chorus 1, with more energy and additional backing vocals.
Bridge — minimal instrumentation, clap rhythm, almost a cappella:
"Будь добрым ты во всём, (Be kind in everything,)
Будь дружней со всеми! (Be friendlier with everyone!)
Мы друзья навеки!" (We are friends forever!) 
Final Chorus — repeat Chorus 1 twice, then final hook line three more times with choir effect.
Outro — laughter, group shout "Друзья!" ("Friends!") fade out.

Instruments: bright synths, funky electric guitar, deep sub bass, punchy kick, clap/snare on beats 2 and 4, upbeat hi-hats, shakers.
Mixing: lead vocal +2dB above instrumental, stereo width on backing vocals, slight reverb on choruses, balanced for mobile speakers.
